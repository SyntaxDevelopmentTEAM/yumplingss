Our Logo

Our logo originated from our thought of displaying the wide culture of Filipinos displaying that we can elevate a normal Filipino dish into something new and unusual. The character in our logo wearing a dumpling hat, is a playful take on our logo including a fanciful element, along with a Filipinos traditional costume, which is a barong that represents the Chinese - Filipino fusion of the food we offer. The character displays a distinctive approach which will make our brand stand out amongst other brands. We used the colors red for enhancing the customers appetite and the yellow color to grab the attention in accordance with The Psychology of Colors in Marketing. The logo, featuring a character in traditional Filipino attire and a unique dumpling hat, combines cultural symbols and culinary imagery to symbolize the integration of Filipino heritage and identity. The red background color symbolizes energy and elegance, while the yellow brand name adds brightness and captures the attention. This combination increases brand recognition and connects the logo with local heritage, expressing authenticity and pride. The logo aims to illustrate the harmonious blend of cultural roots and showcase the rich heritage of Filipino cuisine.
